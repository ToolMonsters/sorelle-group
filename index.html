How We Work

We don't do volume. We do results.

Every search is different. Every client gets our full attention. We don't take on more than we can handle — because when we commit, we see it through to the end.

Connected

Our relationships are our currency. We've spent over three decades building a network across industries, companies, and continents — and we invest in keeping it alive. When you work with us, you get access to people who aren't on job boards, because they don't need to be.

Resourceful

We go further than the database. Direct headhunting, curated referrals, deep industry knowledge and the kind of persistence most recruiters give up on. If the right person exists, we will find them — wherever they are.

Global

Headquartered in North America with active placements across the globe. The best candidate for your role might not live next door in the end. Distance has never been a barrier to the right fit for us.
